WELCOME!!

Summer moon coffee’s Coffee is a 100% crowd-sourced and independently funded community dream come true. Built inside Alton’s historic Milton Schoolhouse, Maeva’s is constructed to embody the exciting collision of culture during the Age of Exploration. The shop is located inside the larger revitalization project: The Milton Schoolhouse here.

Have an office meeting, board game tournament, or a small get together to plan? Call us 48hrs in advance and reserve our gorgeous walnut communal table (seat 10). Reserve the shop for larger events (up to 30 guests) after hours on Sunday from 5pm-8pm. One of our lead baristas will personally attend to your party- making sure every beverage is absolute perfection. $40 per hr + beverages/food.

MENU !!

We now roast our own coffee in house so you get the freshest craft coffee available in Alton.

Our constantly changing assortment of baked goods are sourced from our incubator kitchen, located just below Maeva’s inside The Milton Schoolhouse.

Don’t see it on the menu? Ask. We enjoy a good challenge.
